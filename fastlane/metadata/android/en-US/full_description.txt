<b>Your Music, Your Rhythm</b>

Open-source Material You music player with audiophile-grade audio, zero tracking, and zero ads.

<b>🔊 Sound That Moves You</b>
• Bit Perfect Playback — pure audio sent direct to your hardware
• 10-Band Equalizer + 6,000+ AutoEQ device presets
• Gapless playback, EAC3-JOC/Dolby Atmos codec support
• FLAC, ALAC, MP3, AAC, Opus, WAV, OGG & more

<b>🎨 Design That Delights</b>
• Material 3 Expressive — adaptive shapes, fluid motion
• Material You dynamic colors from your wallpaper (Android 12+)
• Album-art-driven player colors & immersive lyrics view
• Home screen widgets with responsive Material 3 layouts

<b>📚 Library Power</b>
• Multi-select batch actions — queue, share, delete at once
• Shuffle whole albums in correct track order
• Full-scan media scanner — reliable, no songs missed
• Songs, Albums, Artists, Genres, Playlists, Folders

<b>🎤 Lyrics & Metadata</b>
• Synced lyrics via LRCLib with word-by-word highlighting
• Local lyrics editor for custom offsets and edits
• Full metadata editor with artwork support

<b>⚙️ Power Features</b>
• Sleep timer, queue management, M3U import/export
• Settings search, playback stats, Android Auto
• Deezer & YouTube Music artwork integration
• In-app auto-updates from GitHub

<b>🔒 Privacy First</b>
No ads. No analytics. No accounts. 100% offline-capable. GPL v3.
